## Summary

The v0.3 cheatsheet is now a usable research-backed scaffold for authoring and QA, but it is still not at the Kuku Yalanji reference level. The main missing detail is no longer raw topic coverage; most major topics are named and several high-risk gaps now have operational guardrails. The remaining gaps are paradigm completeness, example density, machine-checkable rules, and community/speaker validation.

- **Loop 17 found and fixed a real, project-wide bug**: every one of the 105 internal cross-reference links in the cheatsheet (e.g. `[10.1](#101-full-present-indicative-...)`) was silently broken in the rendered HTML.
